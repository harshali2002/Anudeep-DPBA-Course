Part 1 -> The decision maker

1. The gate keeper (IF)
if(logical test, value true, value false)

Part 2 -> Complex Decisions
AND -> Must meet all expectations.
OR -> Must meet at least one expectation.
NOT -> Opposite of the expectation.

Part 3 -> Text Extractor - LEFT,RIGHT,MID

Left -> get text from left side of the text.
Right -> get text from right side of the text.
Mid -> get text from the middle.

Part 4 -> Joining and formatting (CONCAT, LEN, TRIM)

1. Combining text
CONCAT(text1,text2...)
& - operator

2. Clean-up Crew (LEN, TRIM, PROPER)

LEN - returns number of characters in text.
TRIM - Cleans up the extra white space.
PROPER - converts into proper case, every first letter capital for all words.

TRIM is the most used text function.
